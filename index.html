WORDS OF REMEMBRANCE

A glimpse into the wisdom waiting within The Forgotten You.

❝

When work takes away your stillness, it is no longer work. It is spiritual theft.

❝

Your potential feels trapped in a glass jar. Despite your loyalty, hard work and dreams — you feel unseen. Undervalued. Unfulfilled.

❝

It is not your name. Not your job. Not your past or your hopes. It is not your thoughts, nor the feelings that rise and fall within. The Self is the silent witness behind it all.

❝

The Self is not something to become. It is something to remember. It is your original face before the world told you who you should be.

❝

The one who knows the Self plays the song of life with effortless grace.

These are not merely ideas to understand. They are truths to remember.
